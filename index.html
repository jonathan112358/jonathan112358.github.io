Track Your Film Rolls

Never lose track of your film rolls again. LabMap helps you organize your analog photography workflow by keeping detailed records of each roll, including film stock, camera used, and development status. Stay on top of your creative process with our intuitive tracking system.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2024

LabMap respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Email address (required to log in)
  • Name (optional, if added to your profile)
  • User content, such as rated shops and film development entries

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide you with core app features (e.g., login, profile, saving ratings)
  • Your data is not sold or shared with third parties

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is securely stored and managed through Supabase
  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us
